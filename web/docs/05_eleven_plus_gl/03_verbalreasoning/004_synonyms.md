# Closest Meaning

## **TYPE 4: Synonyms (IPS Type D)**

## **What you are asked to do**

You are given **two groups of words** (usually 2–3 words in each group).

* Choose **ONE word from the first group**
* Choose **ONE word from the second group**
* The two chosen words must be the **closest in meaning**
* **Mark BOTH words** on the answer sheet

> ⚠️ Important:
> You are NOT choosing opposites.
> You are choosing the **most similar meanings**.

---

## **How to solve (step-by-step)**

1. Read **all the words carefully**
2. Think about what each word **really means**
3. Look for:

   * synonyms (same or very similar meaning)
   * strength of meaning (strong vs weak)
4. Pick the **closest possible pair**
5. Ignore words that are only *loosely* related

---

## **Top Tips for 11+**

* 📚 Don’t rush — small meaning differences matter
* 🔍 Ask: *“Could these two words replace each other in a sentence?”*
* ❌ Avoid words that are only linked by topic
* 🧠 Think about **degree** (e.g. *annoyed* ≠ *furious*)
* ✏️ If unsure, eliminate words you clearly know don’t match
