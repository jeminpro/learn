# Complete the Sum

## **TYPE I: Complete the Sum (IPS Type I)**

### **Description**
In these questions, you are given a numerical equation with one missing number.  
Your task is to find the number that makes the equation correct.

---

### **Strategy**
- Carefully read the equation.
- Work out the value of each side.
- Rearrange the equation if needed.
- Use inverse operations (addition ↔ subtraction, multiplication ↔ division).
- Solve step by step to find the missing number.

---

### **Example**

**Question:**  
26 + 7 = 18 + ?

**Working:**  
Left side: 26 + 7 = 33  
So, 18 + ? = 33  
? = 33 − 18 = 15  

**Answer:**  
15

---

### **Tips**
- Always calculate one side first.
- Keep your working neat to avoid mistakes.
- Double-check by substituting your answer back into the equation.
- Watch out for subtraction errors.

---

### **Common Mistakes**
- Adding instead of subtracting when rearranging.
- Forgetting to balance both sides of the equation.
- Rushing and making arithmetic mistakes.
