# Factors 

A **factor** is a **whole number** that divides another number **exactly**, leaving no remainder.

> Think of factors as ‚Äúperfect pieces‚Äù that fit evenly into the number.

### üßÆ Examples:

* Factors of **12**:
  1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12
  ‚úÖ Because:

  * 12 √∑ 1 = 12
  * 12 √∑ 2 = 6
  * 12 √∑ 3 = 4
