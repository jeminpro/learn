#  Phonics
 
## 🩵 **DAYS 1-14 – PRE-PHONICS READINESS**

---

### 🩵 **Day 2 – Rhythm Rocker 🥁**

**Goal:** Hear beats in words

* 👂 Clap names (“El-sa” 2 claps)
* 🪄 Drum the syllables of toys (“Ti-ger-y”)
* 🎲 Move one step per word in a rhyme
  ✅ **Today I can …** clap beats in 3 names
  🧠 *Tip:* Use big movements so the body feels the beats.

---

### 🩵 **Day 3 – Sound Detective 🕵️‍♀️**

**Goal:** Tell same vs different sounds

* 🪄 Play pairs: *sun–sock* vs *dog–moon*
* 👂 Say “Yes!” if they start the same
* 🎲 Sort picture cards into “same” / “different” baskets
  ✅ **Today I can …** hear when words start alike
  🧠 *Tip:* Exaggerate the first sound: “ssssun, ssssock.”

---

### 🩵 **Day 4 – Noise Ninja 🎧**

**Goal:** Discriminate long/short and loud/soft

* 🪄 Bang two objects – which is louder?
* 🎲 Make long snake sound “shhhhh,” short dot “t.”
* 👂 Draw lines (long = snake, short = dot) as you listen.
  ✅ **Today I can …** show long vs short sounds
  🧠 *Tip:* Visuals (🐍/•) help her see sound length.

---

### 🩵 **Day 5 – Mouth Movers 🐍**

**Goal:** Control mouth sounds

* ✏️ Copy you: hiss sss, hum mmm, buzz zzz
* 🪄 Play “mouth gym” – raspberries, tongue pops
* 🎲 Alternate mmm/sss to feel differences
  ✅ **Today I can …** make 3 different mouth sounds
  🧠 *Tip:* Say “Feel where your lips or teeth are!”

---

### 🩵 **Day 6 – Animal Echo 🐾**

**Goal:** Connect sound and source

* 🪄 Make animal noises together (sss snake, mmm cow)
* 🎲 Play guessing game: “You hear mmm – which animal?”
* 👂 Stretch and imitate the sound length (sssslong!)
  ✅ **Today I can …** copy animal sounds accurately
  🧠 *Tip:* Exaggerate duration – it teaches sound stretch.

---

### 🩵 **Day 7 – Alliteration Fun 🎶**

**Goal:** Notice same starting sounds naturally

* 🪄 Say silly strings “sssilly sssocks ssswing”
* 👂 Chant with actions (snap, stamp, sing)
* 🎲 Find another word starting the same
  ✅ **Today I can …** join in a funny sound chant
  🧠 *Tip:* Don’t explain the rule – just repeat sounds.

---

### 💚 **Day 15 – Robot Talk 🤖**

**Goal:** Blend two sounds

* 🪄 Say */a-t/* slowly → ask “What word?”
* 👂 Tap each sound on finger, then slide hand to blend
* 🎲 Play robot voice for 2-sound words (*up, in, on*)
  ✅ **Today I can …** blend 2-sound words with help
  🧠 *Tip:* Keep pace slow and stretch the vowels.

---

### 💚 **Day 16 – Robot Treasure Hunt 🧱**

**Goal:** Blend 3-sound words orally

* 🪄 Hide objects; give sound clues */p-e-n/*
* 👂 She finds matching item
* 🎲 Use toy blocks – push one for each sound then sweep hand
  ✅ **Today I can …** hear 3 sounds and guess word
  🧠 *Tip:* Use real toys so it feels like play not test.

---

### 💚 **Day 17 – Meet the Letters ✏️**

**Goal:** Link sound → letter

* 👂 Teach s a t p i n using pure sounds
* 🪄 Trace each in sand or rice while saying sound
* 🎲 Find objects starting with each sound
  ✅ **Today I can …** say and trace 3 new letters
  🧠 *Tip:* Say sound not letter name.

---

### 💚 **Day 18 – Build and Blend 🧩**

**Goal:** Combine seeing and hearing sounds

* 🪄 Write *sat, pin, tap*; dot under each letter (sound buttons)
* 👂 Press dot as you say sound, then sweep to blend
* 🎲 Make words with magnetic letters
  ✅ **Today I can …** blend 3-letter words with dots
  🧠 *Tip:* Finger tracking is key for left→right direction.

---

### 💚 **Day 19 – Switch-It Game 🎲**

**Goal:** Hear sound changes in words

* 🪄 Change one letter at a time sat→sit→sip→tip
* 👂 Say aloud each new word
* ✏️ Write the new one on mini whiteboard
  ✅ **Today I can …** hear and say changed sound
  🧠 *Tip:* Highlight mouth movement difference.

---

### 💚 **Day 20 – Story Sounds 📚**

**Goal:** Read simple CVC words in context

* 👂 Watch Alphablocks Ep 1–5, pause and blend together
* 🪄 Build silly sentences (*The cat sat on a pin!*)
* 🎲 Draw what she hears */s-u-n/* → sun
  ✅ **Today I can …** blend in short sentences
  🧠 *Tip:* Keep humour – funny sentences cement memory.

---

### 💚 **Day 21 – Mini Phonics Champion 🎉**

**Goal:** Fluency and celebration

* 🪄 Quick Blend Challenge – how many words in 1 min?
* 👂 Read one mini book (Oxford Owl Stage 1+)
* 🎲 Recap game: “I’ll say it slow / you say it fast!”
  ✅ **Today I can …** read CVC words confidently!
  🧠 *Tip:* End with praise – confidence is the real goal.
