# Drill -Subject Verb Agreement

---

# **SECTION A — Basic SVA (10 questions)**

Choose the correct verb.

1. The cat (run / **runs**) very fast.
2. My friends (is / **are**) here.
3. He (eat / **eats**) breakfast early.
4. They (play / **play**) in the park.
5. She (walk / **walks**) to school every day.
6. The dogs (bark / **bark**) loudly.
7. I (likes / **like**) reading comics.
8. You (was / **were**) right.
9. The baby (cry / **cries**) often.
10. The girls (dance / **dance**) beautifully.

---

# **SECTION B — Indefinite Pronouns (8 questions)**

(everyone, nobody, somebody, each, every = singular)

11. Everyone (is / **is**) ready.
12. Nobody (want / **wants**) to leave.
13. Each child (was / **was**) given a badge.
14. Someone (has / **has**) taken my pen.
15. Everybody (love / **loves**) music.
16. Each of the players (is / **is**) skilled.
17. Nobody (know / **knows**) the answer.
18. Every student (needs / **needs**) a pencil.

---

# **SECTION C — Prepositional Phrases (7 questions)**

Ignore: “of”, “with”, “along with”, “together with”, “beside”, etc.

19. The box **of chocolates** (is / are) missing.
20. The group **of friends** (was / were) laughing.
21. The pile **of books** (is / are) heavy.
22. The boys **with the new shoes** (is / **are**) excited.
23. The bouquet **of roses** (is / are) beautiful.
24. The teacher **with the two assistants** (is / are) strict.
25. The basket **of apples** (was / were) full.

---

# **SECTION D — And / Or / Nor (8 questions)**

### **A. Subjects joined by AND → plural**

26. Tom and Jerry (is / **are**) funny.
27. A pencil and a ruler (was / **were**) on the table.

### **B. Either/Or & Neither/Nor → verb matches the **closer** subject**

28. Either the boys or the teacher (is / **is**) coming.
29. Either the teacher or the boys (is / **are**) coming.
30. Neither the dog nor the cats (is / **are**) hungry.
31. Neither the cats nor the dog (is / are) hungry.
32. Either my parents or my sister (is / **is**) arriving.
33. Either my sister or my parents (is / **are**) arriving.

---

# **SECTION E — Collective Nouns (7 questions)**

(team, family, group, committee = singular)

34. The team (is / are) winning.
35. My family (is / are) going on holiday.
36. The committee (has / have) decided.
37. The crowd (was / were) cheering.
38. The class (is / are) taking a test.
39. The band (plays / play) well together.
40. The audience (was / were) delighted.

---

# **SECTION F — Tricky Irregular Verbs (10 questions)**

(has/have, is/are, was/were, does/do, goes/go, says/say, gives/give)

41. She (has / have) a red bicycle.
42. They (has / **have**) a red bicycle.
43. He (does / do) his homework at night.
44. They (does / **do**) their homework at night.
45. She (go / **goes**) to gymnastics.
46. The children (go / **go**) to gymnastics.
47. He (say / **says**) he is tired.
48. They (say / **say**) they are tired.
49. She (give / **gives**) her friend a gift.
50. The teachers (give / **give**) homework on Fridays.
