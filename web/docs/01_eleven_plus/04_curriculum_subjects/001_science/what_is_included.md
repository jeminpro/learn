# What is included?

# 🟩 1. Living Things & Life Processes (VERY HIGH PRIORITY)

## Core Concepts

### a) Characteristics of Living Things

* Move
* Grow
* Reproduce
* Respire (release energy)
* Respond (react to environment)

👉 Often simplified as **life processes**

---

### b) Plants vs Animals

#### Plants:

* make their own food (**photosynthesis**)
* need sunlight, water, carbon dioxide

#### Animals:

* eat other organisms

---

### Key Vocabulary

* **organism**
* **living / non-living**
* **respiration**
* **reproduction**
* **photosynthesis** ⭐ (very important)
* **nutrients**

---

### Likely Questions

* “Process by which plants make food?” → **Photosynthesis**
* “Living things are called?” → **Organisms**
* “What gas do plants use?” → **Carbon dioxide**

---

# ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

Don’t teach:
❌ chemical equations
❌ deep biology (cells, DNA detail)
❌ complex physics formulas

Too deep = wasted effort for FSCE.

---

# 🎯 How to Teach (Best Method)

Since it's MCQ / one-word:

### Step 1: Teach word + meaning

### Step 2: Immediate recall

### Step 3: MCQ practice

Example:

* Q: “What is a push or pull?” → **Force**

---

6. Energy
7. Earth & space
8. Scientific skills

* **concept-light but precise**
