# Art & Design

## 📂 Structure Used

* Observation & description
* Colour & emotion
* Shape, form & composition
* Materials & techniques
* Artists & styles (conceptual, not memorisation-heavy)
* Evaluation & improvement
* Design choices & reasoning
* Real-life application

# 🚀 HOW TO USE THIS (IMPORTANT FOR 11+)

Art is NOT about drawing skill—it is about:

* explaining choices
* justifying opinions
* evaluating quality
* linking visuals to meaning

Always ask:

* “Why did you choose that?”
* “What does it show?”
* “How could it be better?”

👉 This builds **high-level reasoning + verbal explanation skills**
