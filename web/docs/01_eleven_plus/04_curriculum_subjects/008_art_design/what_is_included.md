# What is included?

## 1. 🎨 Elements of Art (VERY HIGH PRIORITY)

This is the **most important section** — very testable.

### Teach these core elements:

* **Line** → marks (straight, curved, zigzag)
* **Shape** → 2D forms (circle, square)
* **Form** → 3D shapes (cube, sphere)
* **Colour** → hues and tones
* **Texture** → how something feels or looks like it feels
* **Pattern** → repeated design
* **Space** → area around or within objects

---

### Key Vocabulary

* line, shape, form
* colour, texture
* pattern, space

---

### Likely Questions

* “A repeated design is called?” → **Pattern**
* “A 3D shape is called?” → **Form**
* “How something feels is called?” → **Texture**

---

## 2. 🌈 Colour Theory (HIGH PRIORITY)

Very MCQ-friendly.

### Teach:

#### Primary Colours

* **Red, Blue, Yellow**

#### Secondary Colours

* **Green, Orange, Purple**

#### Colour Mixing

* Red + Blue → **Purple**
* Blue + Yellow → **Green**
* Red + Yellow → **Orange**

---

### Vocabulary

* **primary**
* **secondary**
* **mix**
* **shade** (add black → darker)
* **tint** (add white → lighter)

---

### Likely Questions

* “Which are primary colours?” → **Red, Blue, Yellow**
* “Red + Yellow makes?” → **Orange**
* “Adding white makes a colour lighter. This is called?” → **Tint**

---

### Likely Questions

# 🎯 What FSCE Will Actually Test

They WON’T ask:
❌ Famous artists (usually)
❌ Art history details
❌ Complex techniques

They WILL ask:
✅ Vocabulary meaning
✅ Colour mixing
✅ Identify elements (pattern, texture etc.)
✅ Simple definitions

# 🧠 Smart Teaching Tip (Important)

Since your child is strong in logic:

👉 Turn art into **thinking questions**

Example:

* “Why is pattern useful?” → repetition, design
* “Why do we use proportion?” → makes drawing realistic
