# What is included?
## 1. 🎶 Elements of Music (VERY HIGH PRIORITY)

This is the **most important section** — extremely testable.

### Teach these core elements:

* **Pitch** → how high or low a sound is
* **Volume (Dynamics)** → how loud or quiet
* **Tempo** → how fast or slow
* **Rhythm** → pattern of beats
* **Pulse (Beat)** → steady beat
* **Timbre** → sound quality (what makes instruments sound different)
* **Duration** → how long a note lasts

---

### Key Vocabulary

* pitch, volume
* tempo, rhythm
* pulse / beat
* timbre
* duration

---

### Likely Questions

* “How fast or slow music is called?” → **Tempo**
* “High or low sound is called?” → **Pitch**
* “Steady beat is called?” → **Pulse**
* “What makes a piano sound different from a drum?” → **Timbre**

---

# 🎯 What FSCE Will Actually Test

They WON’T ask:
❌ Music history
❌ Famous composers
❌ Reading sheet music in detail

They WILL ask:
✅ Definitions (tempo, pitch, rhythm)
✅ Identify sound features
✅ Instrument groups
✅ Simple music vocabulary

# 🧠 Smart Teaching Tip

Make it **interactive**:

👉 Clap patterns → ask: “Is this rhythm or beat?”
👉 Play fast vs slow → ask: “What changed?” → **Tempo**

This helps memory much more than just definitions.
