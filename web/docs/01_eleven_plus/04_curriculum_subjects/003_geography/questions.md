# Geography

## 📂 Structure Used

Each topic includes:

* WHY
* WHAT IF
* HOW
* COMPARE
* REAL LIFE
* DECISION

# 🚀 How to Use This for 11+ Coaching

For EACH question:

1. Get a full answer
2. Then push deeper:

   * “Why exactly?”
   * “What’s your evidence?”
   * “Can you give a real-life example?”

👉 This builds **reasoning, not memorisation**

---

# ⚠️ Pro Tip (This is what makes your system powerful)

Turn 1 question into 3 levels:

Example:

* Basic: Why do rivers flood?
* Intermediate: How can flooding be reduced?
* Advanced: Should people be allowed to build near rivers?
