# ⚠️ What NOT to Spend Time On

Avoid:
❌ Detailed country facts
❌ Capital cities memorisation
❌ Case studies (volcanoes, earthquakes in detail)

Low return for FSCE.

---

# 🎯 What FSCE Will Actually Test

They will test:
✅ vocabulary (climate, population, evaporation)
✅ basic understanding
✅ map skills (directions, symbols)
✅ simple processes (water cycle)

---

# 🧠 Teaching Strategy (Important)

Use **quick recall style**:

* “What is a large landmass?” → Continent
* “Which direction is opposite North?” → South

4. Physical features
