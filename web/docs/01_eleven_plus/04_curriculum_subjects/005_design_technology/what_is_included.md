# ⚠️ What NOT to Teach

Avoid:
❌ detailed construction methods
❌ specific projects (bridges, houses etc.)
❌ advanced engineering

Low return for FSCE.

---

# 🎯 What FSCE Will Actually Test

They will test:
✅ understanding of materials
✅ purpose of tools
✅ design process
✅ simple mechanics
✅ vocabulary
