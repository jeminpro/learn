Example:
$$

Steps:

## [5. Algebra Basics: The Distributive Property](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S3IEeCyUWWA)

### ğŸ“˜ Overview

This lesson introduces the **distributive property**, which lets you multiply a number across terms inside parentheses:

$$
a(b + c) = ab + ac
$$

Used when:

* You **cannot** simplify inside the parentheses
* You need to **expand** expressions
* You want to remove parentheses when solving equations

The video also shows how to **factor** (reverse distribution):

$$
12x + 6 = 6(2x + 1)
$$

It emphasises careful handling of negative signs.
