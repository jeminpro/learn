### üß† Strategy:

1. **Read carefully**: What‚Äôs the total? What part?
2. **Underline keywords**: ‚Äúof‚Äù, ‚Äúleft‚Äù, ‚Äúshared‚Äù, ‚Äúeach gets‚Äù, ‚Äúmore than‚Äù, etc.

### üßÆ Examples:

1. Sam eats $\tfrac{2}{5}$ of a 30cm chocolate bar. How much did he eat?
   ‚Üí $\tfrac{2}{5}$ √ó 30 = **12cm**

2. A jug holds 1 litre. You drink $\tfrac{3}{4}$ of it and your friend drinks $\tfrac{1}{4}$. How much is left?
   ‚Üí 1 ‚àí ($\tfrac{3}{4}$ + $\tfrac{1}{4}$) = **0L** (none left)

3. A cake is $\tfrac{2}{3}$ kg. You divide it equally between 4 people.
   ‚Üí $\tfrac{2}{3}$ √∑ 4 = $\tfrac{2}{3}$ √ó $\tfrac{1}{4}$ = **$\tfrac{2}{12}$ = $\tfrac{1}{6}$ kg each**

### üß© Word Problems:

#### üîπ Easy
