# Two odd ones out

## **TYPE 2: Two Odd Ones Out (IPS Type B)**

## **What you are asked to do**

You are given **five words**.

* **Three words belong together** (they share something in common)
* **Two words do NOT fit**
* You must **find and mark BOTH odd ones out**

> ⚠️ Very important:
> You are not looking for *one* odd word — **you must find the two that do not belong with the other three**.

---

## **How to solve (step-by-step)**

1. **Read all five words carefully**
2. Ask yourself:

   * Are some the **same type**? (colour, animal, job, object, action, etc.)
3. **Group the three that clearly belong together**
4. The **two left over** are your answer
5. Double-check:

   * Do the three match for the **same reason**?

---

## **Top Tips for 11+**

* 🧠 The link is often:

  * category (animals, tools, colours)
  * function (things you eat, wear, use)
  * grammar (verbs vs nouns)
  * meaning (synonyms / opposites)
* 👀 Don’t rush — **two answers are needed**
* ❌ Don’t choose just the “most different” word
* 📚 If stuck, ask: *“Which three could I explain with one sentence?”*
