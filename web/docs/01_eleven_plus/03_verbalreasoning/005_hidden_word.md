# Hidden word

### Short “how to”

A **4-letter word** is hidden **across two words that sit next to each other** in the sentence.

### Steps

1. Read the sentence once normally.
2. Scan **pairs of neighbouring words** (word1 + word2).
3. Look for **4 letters in a row** that make a real word, using the **end of word1 + start of word2**.
4. Mark the **two words** that contain it.

### Tips

* The hidden word is **continuous** (no gaps, no extra letters).
* The answer is the **pair of words**, not just the hidden word.
* If you spot a hidden word, quickly check the other word pairs don’t also create a real 4-letter word.

## Practice Questions (30)

Find the **pair of words** hiding a 4-letter word.

1. The rabbit curled in the nest quietly.

2. We found this shell on the beach.

3. I saw that cat again today.

4. He solved it with wit help from his friend.

5. Please move right a little.

6. Please pin to the noticeboard.

7. Place the file in the red folder.

8. A spider sat on ceiling corner.

9. They will annex the small field.

10. Please echo me if you agree.

11. Beauty is skin deep.

12. We will help lay the tiles.

13. It was hidden in side pocket.

14. He promised for ever to try his best.

15. We waited near east gate.

16. She gave broad advice to the class.

17. A human youth learns quickly.

18. The top athlete won the race.

19. Please separate each pile neatly.

20. He spilled the tea mug.

21. They agreed on a new order.

22. We visited the far market.

23. His late arrival annoyed us.

24. A wide aisle helps queues move.

25. We played music old and new.

26. Ensure everyone listens carefully.

27. We will reach higher goals soon.

28. The attic was dusty.

29. Children learn now quickly.

30. Don’t mistake easily what you hear.
