# Number Series

### What you are asked to do

You are given a **number sequence**.
Your job is to **find the next number** by spotting the pattern.

Example:
2 4 6 8 10 ( ? )
Pattern: +2 each time
Answer: **12**

---

## How to solve (step-by-step)

1. Look at the **difference** between numbers.
2. Ask yourself:

   * Is it adding or subtracting?
   * Is it multiplying or dividing?
   * Does it alternate?
   * Are two patterns happening at once?
3. Apply the same rule to find the **next number**.
4. Check the answer feels **simple and sensible** (11+ rarely uses very complex maths).

---

## Common patterns to expect in 11+

* ➕ Adding or subtracting a fixed number
* ✖️ Multiplying or dividing
* 🔁 Alternating patterns (e.g. +2, +4, +2, +4)
* 🔢 Increasing steps (e.g. +1, +2, +3…)
* 👯 Two sequences mixed together (odd/even positions)
* ⬆️ Squares or simple times tables
