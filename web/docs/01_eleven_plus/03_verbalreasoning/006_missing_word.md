# Missing word

### What you are asked to do

A word in **CAPITALS** has had **3 consecutive letters removed**.
Those **3 missing letters** (in the same order) form a **real 3-letter word**.
When you put them back, the **sentence must make sense**.

Example: The children **GATED** in the hall.
gat **HER** ed → **GATHERED**
Answer: **HER**

---

## How to solve (quick method)

1. Read the sentence and guess the **intended full word**.
2. Compare it to the CAPITAL word and spot what’s missing.
3. The missing part is **exactly 3 letters in a row**.
4. Check:

   * the 3 letters make a **real 3-letter word**
   * the completed word is spelled correctly
   * the sentence makes sense
