# Compound words

### What you are asked to do

Pick **one word from Group 1** and **one word from Group 2**.
Put them together (Group 1 word **first**) to make **one correctly spelt word**.
✅ **No rearranging letters.**

Example: (hand finger toe) (sum some sun)
hand + some = **handsome**
Answer: **hand**, **some**

---

## How to solve (step-by-step)

1. Try combining a word from Group 1 with each word in Group 2.
2. Say it out loud — does it sound like a real word?
3. Check spelling and meaning.
4. Mark **both** words.

---

## Tips

* Common second parts: **-time, -day, -board, -proof, -house, -work, -light, -side**
* Watch spelling traps: **sun + rise** (not “sunrize”), **note + book** (not “notebook” is correct)
* Don’t pick two “real words” — you need **one joined word**.
