# Opposite Meaning

### What you are asked to do

You are given **two groups of words**.

* Choose **ONE word from the first group**
* Choose **ONE word from the second group**
* The two chosen words must be the **most opposite in meaning**
* **Mark BOTH words**

Example:
(good luck today) (wish charm bad)
→ **good** is opposite of **bad**
Answer: **good, bad**

---

## How to solve (step-by-step)

1. Read **all the words** carefully.
2. Look for a word in the second group that is the **clearest opposite** of a word in the first group.
3. Check strength of meaning (e.g. *hot* vs *freezing* is stronger than *hot* vs *cool*).
4. Ignore words that are only **slightly different** or just unrelated.
5. Choose the **strongest opposite pair**.

---

## Tips for 11+

* 🔄 Opposites are often about:

  * size (big ↔ small)
  * speed (fast ↔ slow)
  * emotion (happy ↔ sad)
  * direction (up ↔ down)
  * quality (safe ↔ dangerous)
* ⚠️ Don’t mix up **different categories** (e.g. noun vs adjective).
* ❌ Avoid pairs that are just “not the same” — they must be **true opposites**.
