# Insert a Letter

## **TYPE 1: Insert a Letter (IPS Type A)**

### **What you are asked to do**

You are given **two pairs of brackets**.
The **same single letter** must:

1. **Finish** the word before the brackets
2. **Start** the word after the brackets

So you are really making **four real words** using **one shared letter**.

---

### **How to solve (simple steps for children)**

1. **Look at the first word** and think:
   *What letter could come at the end to make a real word?*
2. **Try the same letter** at the start of the next word.
3. **Check all four words** are real words.
4. If one word doesn’t work → **try a different letter**.

> ✅ The correct answer always makes **all four** words real.

---

### **Top Tips for 11+**

* 🔤 Try **common letters first**: **t, s, r, l, n**
* 🧠 Say the words **out loud** — your ear often catches mistakes faster than your eyes
* ❌ If **even one word isn’t real**, the letter is wrong
* ⏱️ Don’t overthink — most answers are **simple everyday words**
* 📌 Pencil the letter lightly and test it mentally before committing
